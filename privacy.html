Privacy Policy

Social Publisher is a private desktop application used by its owner. Authentication is completed on each platform's official website. The application stores authorization tokens locally on the owner's computer so connected accounts can be reused.

Social Publisher does not sell personal information, does not display advertising, and does not share account data with unrelated third parties. Media selected by the owner is transmitted only to the platforms the owner selects.

The owner can remove saved accounts inside the application and can revoke access through each platform's security settings.
